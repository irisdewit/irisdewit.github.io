    About Me

    Driven by a curiosity and genuine enthusiasm for diverse experiences, I thrive on exploring new things. Hence, I enjoy practising many types of sports, making music, learning, cooking and preferably together with people around me. Study wise, I found my interest in both psychology and technology. Following this path, User Experience and Interface (UX & UI) got me very excited. Central to my design philosophy is the importance of the user. Understanding their perspectives, behaviors, and preferences is at the core of my approach to user-centered design. With my problem-solving attitude, I enjoy developing solutions that aim to meet their user's needs and expectations. I believe in technology, as long as it serves the human needs. Deploying technology to make the world just a bit better by helping (groups of) people, through making technology accessible, or by using it to create a more sustainable world. I would like to work in projects that embody these values, and contribute in a team by applying my skills from user-research to prototyping to front-end design.
