    About Me

    As an open-minded and curious individual, I tend to be in the middle of different interests. One of these middle fields is the intersection of psychology and technology. Over the years, I've been captivated by the large influence technology has in our society nowadays, and our ability to design technology that serves everyone. My journey through the realms of psychology and technology has equipped me with a deep understanding of human behavior and the pivotal role technology plays in our lives. My commitment lies in applying this knowledge to build a world where sustainability and social responsibility take center stage. I'm not content with simply designing interfaces or crafting user experiences; I aspire to leverage my expertise to contribute positively to society. My ultimate goal is to wield technology as a force for good, propelling us toward a more sustainable and inclusive future. With an unwavering focus on ethical and social considerations, I'm dedicated to ensuring that every digital experience I create reflects my values and contributes to a better world.
